## Headline you're aiming for
> *"12 quants shipped 28 skills/agents; median 3 hrs/week saved; 80% still active at 30 days."*

That sentence beats any satisfaction survey. Collect the inputs during the cohort (demo day) and
at the T+30 check-in (one-line email — template in the [facilitator guide](facilitator-guide.md)).

## What NOT to over-measure
- Satisfaction scores (vanity).
- Lines of code / number of prompts (not outcomes).
Keep it to: did they ship something real, and is it saving time 30 days later.
